Aquatic Sonnet

Water has always fascinated me.
I feel most content when I’m by the sea,
Or when sunsets color the river stream.
On many occasions in my dreams,
A flood has taken over my dream-world.
No matter what kind of plot has unfurled,
People swim instead of walking to locations,
Stairways are waterfalls and chairs are flotations,
And for whatever reason, I don’t care.
I’d move to somewhere coastal if I dared
To leave New York, but I’m fine with staying.
Though the Hudson River keeps on decaying,
I still think it’s lovely with city light
Colors painted on the ripples at night.
