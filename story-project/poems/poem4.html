One "S"

I would not be Marisa if my brother wasn’t Matt.
My mom thought it would be cute to have “M&M” twins.
She also made the bold decision to spell my name
With one “S.”

Apparently my name is supposed to be spelled with two,
Because not a single teacher, friend, or acquaintance
Got the memo that I spell my name
With one “S.”

I’ve been called Marissa, Marrissa, Merissa,
Even Mariska (Hargitay) or Maritza (by a barista).
Anything but Marisa
With one “S.”

It’s even been pronounced incorrectly;
It’s muh-RISS-uh, not mah-REES-uh.
Why is it so hard? Everyone knows a Marisa
With one “S.”

Ironically, the name Marisa has left the vocabulary
Of everyone closest to me. To my family and friends,
I’m Ris or Mar; there’s no need to remember to spell Marisa
With one “S.”
