2. Keep Yourself Busy

As much as you probably don't want to do anything but sit there and be sad, that does nothing for your healing process! Get up! Keep your mind busy. Take up a new hobby, go out of the house and explore... anything that will help you feel confident about yourself and stop thinking about your ex!

Some fun ideas to help keep your mind busy: Find a new exercise routine, paint a picture, get a haircut, start a new book to read, take a class, etc.
